## Core Platform Architecture

### Copy Maker: Dynamic Card-Based Interface

CopyZap's Copy Maker revolutionizes content creation with a card-based workflow that provides unprecedented flexibility and control.

**Key Advantages:**

- **On-Demand Generation**: Create alternatives, apply voice styles, or generate quality scores for any content piece without regenerating everything
- **Visual Threading**: Clear visual connections show relationships between original content and derived variations
- **Independent Actions**: Each generated content card has its own action buttons for targeted improvements
- **Progressive Enhancement**: Start with basic copy and selectively develop only the pieces that need further refinement
- **Real-Time Progress Tracking**: Detailed progress messages display as content generates, with operation cancellation support
- **Content Preservation**: All generated content remains accessible until you choose to clear it
- **Cost Efficiency**: Generate only what you need, when you need it, saving on API costs

**Why Card-Based Design Matters:**

Traditional copywriting tools force you to regenerate everything when you want variations. Copy Maker eliminates this waste by letting you selectively enhance only the content pieces you want to develop further. This approach:
- Saves time by avoiding unnecessary regeneration
- Reduces API costs significantly
- Gives you complete creative control
- Allows iterative refinement without starting over

## Special Instructions: The Power Feature

### What Makes Special Instructions Special?

The **Special Instructions** field is CopyZap's most powerful feature, offering unlimited flexibility to customize AI behavior beyond standard form fields. This free-form text field appends directly to your AI prompt, giving you complete control over output characteristics.

**Why It's Powerful:**

1. **Unlimited Customization**: No predefined options limit you
2. **Direct Prompt Control**: Your instructions pass directly to the AI
3. **Handles Edge Cases**: Cover situations standard fields can't address
4. **Multi-Constraint Support**: Combine multiple specific requirements
5. **Creative Freedom**: Experiment with unconventional approaches
6. **Professional Precision**: Achieve exact requirements for demanding clients

### Special Instructions Categories & Examples

#### Regional/Cultural Customization
- "Use Vienna slang and local expressions"
- "Include British spelling throughout"

- "Incorporate Australian humor and cultural references"

- "Use bullet points only, no paragraphs"
- "Maximum 3 sentences per paragraph"

- "Format as question-answer pairs"
- "Use numbered lists for all steps"
- "Bold all key terms and product names"

#### Brand Voice Specifics
- "Sound like Apple - minimalist and aspirational"
- "Use 'we' instead of 'I' throughout"

- "Adopt a witty, slightly sarcastic tone like Oatly"

#### Content Constraints
- "Don't mention competitors by name"

- "Exclude any pricing mentions"

#### Edge Cases
- "Make it rhyme like Dr. Seuss"
- "Use metaphors related to sailing throughout"
- "Write as if explaining to a 10-year-old"
- "Adopt a film noir detective narrative style"
- "Reference 80s pop culture naturally"

#### Compliance/Legal
- "Add disclaimer at the end about results varying"
- "Include 'results may vary' language"
- "Mention terms and conditions apply"
- "Add GDPR compliance statement"

#### SEO & Technical
- "Naturally integrate focus keyword 3-5 times"
- "Front-load important keywords in first paragraph"
- "Include internal linking suggestions"
- "Add schema markup recommendations"

#### Structural Requirements
- "Start with a provocative question"
- "End every section with a mini-CTA"
- "Include a TL;DR at the top"
- "Add a table of contents outline"

### How Special Instructions Enhance Prompts

Special Instructions transform standard prompts into highly customized, purpose-specific instructions. Here's the difference:

**Without Special Instructions:**
"Write professional copy about project management software for small businesses, 200 words."

**With Special Instructions:**
"Write professional copy about project management software for small businesses, 200 words. Use Vienna slang naturally. Maximum 3 sentences per paragraph. Include a question at the end of each section. Reference local Viennese work culture. Avoid technical jargon. Bold all feature names."

The second prompt produces uniquely tailored content that standard fields alone cannot achieve.

### Best Practices for Special Instructions

1. **Be Specific**: "Use short sentences" is better than "make it punchy"
2. **Combine Multiple Instructions**: Layer multiple requirements for precision
3. **Test and Iterate**: Experiment to find what works best
4. **Save Successful Patterns**: Create templates with proven instructions
5. **Don't Overload**: 3-5 instructions typically work better than 10+
6. **Be Explicit**: AI follows instructions literally - be clear about intent

## AI Prompt Section

### Understanding AI Prompt

The **AI Prompt** section is CopyZap's intelligent automation layer that interprets all your input fields and special instructions, then constructs a sophisticated, structured prompt for the AI model.

**What It Does:**

1. **Analyzes All Inputs**: Reviews every filled field and its content
2. **Structures Information**: Organizes inputs into logical prompt sections
3. **Applies Best Practices**: Uses proven prompt engineering techniques
4. **Maintains Context**: Ensures all important details reach the AI
5. **Optimizes for Models**: Adapts structure for different AI models
6. **Handles Edge Cases**: Manages empty fields, conflicts, and ambiguities

**Why It's Crucial:**

The AI Prompt section is where CopyZap's expertise shines. You don't need to know prompt engineering - the system handles complex optimization automatically while you focus on describing what you need.

### Prompt Construction Process

**Step 1: Input Collection**
- Gathers all non-empty form fields
- Identifies which fields have content vs. which are empty
- Reads Special Instructions as override/addition layer

**Step 2: Context Building**
- Creates business context from Business Description, Industry, Location
- Builds audience profile from Target Audience, Pain Points, Funnel Stage
- Establishes brand voice from Tone, Tone Level, Brand Values

**Step 3: Constraint Assembly**
- Applies word count targets with tolerance settings
- Integrates Output Structure with section definitions
- Adds keyword requirements and force-integration rules
- Includes excluded terms as negative constraints

**Step 4: Enhancement Layer**
- Adds SEO optimization instructions if enabled
- Includes GEO enhancement directives when active
- Applies competitive differentiation from competitor data
- Integrates special instructions as final customization layer

**Step 5: Quality Controls**
- Adds word count accuracy requirements
- Includes elaboration and example forcing rules
- Applies structural consistency checks
- Ensures CTA integration

**Step 6: Output Formatting**
- Defines expected output structure
- Specifies section headers and organization
- Sets tone and style consistency requirements

### View Prompts Feature

**Purpose**: Transparency and learning tool

After generating content, you can view the exact prompts sent to the AI:
- **System Prompt**: AI's role, capabilities, and global instructions
- **User Prompt**: Your specific request with all inputs combined

**Benefits:**
1. **Learn Prompt Engineering**: See how professional prompts are structured
2. **Debug Issues**: Identify why output didn't meet expectations
3. **Iterate Improvements**: Understand what changes affect results
4. **Copy for Reuse**: Save successful prompts for other tools
5. **Educational Value**: Understand AI prompt construction

# CopyZap - Quick-Start Templates Library

## Overview

Pre-configured templates for common content types, enabling instant setup without field-by-field configuration.

## How to Use These Templates

**Method 1: Manual Configuration**
1. Copy values from template below
2. Fill corresponding form fields
3. Adjust for your specific needs
4. Generate

**Method 2: Save as Template**
1. Configure based on template
2. Click "Save as Template"
3. Reuse for similar projects

## Landing Page Templates

### Template 1.1: SaaS Landing Page Hero

```yaml
NAME: "SaaS Hero - Professional B2B"

MODE: Create New Copy
AI MODEL: GPT-4 Turbo

"[Your product name] is a [category] solution designed for [target market].
We solve [specific problem] by providing [key features]. Unlike [alternatives],
we focus on [unique approach], making [key benefit] effortless."

PRODUCT/SERVICE NAME: [Your Product Name]

TARGET AUDIENCE:
"[Decision makers] at [company size] [industry] companies who are struggling
with [pain point]. They value [what matters to them] and need [outcome]
within [timeframe]."

TARGET AUDIENCE PAIN POINTS:
"[Pain 1]. [Pain 2]. [Pain 3]. [Current failed solution]. [Consequence of
unsolved problem]."

KEY MESSAGE: "[Single most important benefit or transformation]"

TONE: Professional
TONE LEVEL: 6
WORD COUNT: Custom (150)
LANGUAGE: English

1. Header 1 (30 words)
2. Problem (50 words)
3. Solution (70 words)
4. Call to Action (30 words)

CALL TO ACTION: "Start your free 14-day trial—no credit card required"

SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS:
"Maximum 3 sentences per paragraph. Bold product name on first mention.
Include one compelling metric or statistic. Professional but approachable tone."

GENERATE SEO: YES (2 variants)
GENERATE GEO: YES (Add TL;DR: YES)
GENERATE SCORES: NO initially
```

**Best For:**
- B2B SaaS products
- Professional services
- Enterprise software
- Technical products

## Template Selection Guide

**Choose Template Based On:**

1. **Content Type:** Landing page, blog, email, ad, etc.
2. **Audience:** B2B professional, consumer, technical, general
3. **Tone Needed:** Professional, friendly, bold, luxury
4. **Length:** Short (ads), medium (pages), long (blogs, sales)
5. **Budget:** DeepSeek (economical), GPT-4 Turbo (balanced), GPT-4o (premium)
